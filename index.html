SB Field Management — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

September Bloom USA ("we", "us") operates the SB Field Management mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

Data We Collect

  • Account information — email and authentication credentials, used to sign you in.
  • Photos and videos — media you capture in the App to document field activity.
  • Location — approximate and precise location attached to captured media (when permission is granted).
  • Device and diagnostic data — crash logs, performance metrics, and app usage events, used to improve reliability.

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and authorize access to their organization's content.
  • To upload, store, and display field messages, media, and related metadata.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability and features.

Data Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal data. We share data with the following service providers strictly to operate the App:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — authentication (Cognito), media storage (S3), and API hosting.
  • Google Firebase — crash reporting (Crashlytics) and analytics.

Data Security

All network traffic between the App and our services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Credentials on the device are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences.

Data Retention

Account and content data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the address below.

User Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
